Overview

Purpose

This website will help customers find our business and build revenue. It will give them an idea of what to expect when booking with us.

Audience

The target audience is for groups of young adults who want an exciting weekend adventure. They should be able to find the requirements needed to raft, availablity, and costs. I expect users to access the site mainly through the computer, but often on a mobile device as well.
