History

Founded on the banks of the Truckee River, AquaXtreme Rafting has a rich history rooted in a passion for adventure and a deep love for the outdoors. Established over two decades ago by a group of avid rafters, our company began as a humble endeavor to share the exhilarating experience of white water rafting with others. From our earliest days, we were driven by a commitment to safety, professionalism, and providing unparalleled thrills on the river. Over the years, AquaXtreme has evolved into a premier rafting destination, known for our expert guides, top-of-the-line equipment, and unforgettable rafting experiences. Today, we continue to honor our founding principles while embracing innovation and sustainability, ensuring that future generations can also enjoy the thrill of riding the rapids with AquaXtreme Rafting.
